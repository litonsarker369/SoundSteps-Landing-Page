The challenge many parents face

Speech development is important, but getting the right support shouldn't be so difficult.

18+

Long wait times for NHS support

Many families face incredibly long waits for speech and language therapy through the NHS, leaving parents feeling helpless.

Unsure how to help at home

Parents want to support their children but often don't know what exercises to do or how to make practice effective.

Lack of structured tools

There are few affordable, easy-to-use tools that provide daily structured speech practice for families.

A calm, supportive way to practise speech at home

SoundSteps gives you simple, structured daily exercises that fit easily into your routine. No pressure, just gentle encouragement and clear progress tracking.

  • Simple daily exercises designed by speech specialists
  • Track progress with gentle, encouraging feedback
  • No waiting rooms or appointments - practice anytime
  • Built for parents, regardless of experience

How SoundSteps works

Five simple steps to support your child's speech practice

1

Listen

Play a clear audio clip of the target sound

2

Repeat

Your child tries saying the sound themselves

3

Encourage

Use positive prompts to build confidence

4

Record

Save recordings to track progress over time

5

Progress

See gentle suggestions for next steps

Our Mission

SoundSteps was created because we believe every child deserves the support they need to find their voice. We're building this app because we know how hard it can be for families to access speech support - and because we believe parents are the best advocates for their children.

By joining Early Access, you're not just getting early access to an app. You're becoming part of a community that's shaping how families access speech support. Your insights will directly influence the final product.
